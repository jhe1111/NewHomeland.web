NewHomeland.web

is an interactive digital archive.


NewHomeland is an archival project inquiring into the open-access landscape images that are widely circulated online and frequently used on mass-produced home accessories. Focusing on the process of image reproduction, this project reflects on the mode of production enabled by contemporary Internet culture and highly democratized digital tools, as well as how they influence the creative practices outside of the mainstream visual culture.
